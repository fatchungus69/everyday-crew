Our Story

The things you wear every day should be the best things you own.

Most basics aren't that basic. You're either paying too much for a name, or settling for something that falls apart after a few washes. We here at Everyday Crew wanted to create something that not only felt like you were walking through a cloud — but lasted when it actually mattered.

Everyday Crew was built on a simple idea: quality shouldn't be a luxury. Soft enough to forget you're wearing them. Durable enough to actually last. Minimal enough to go with anything.

We started with socks — because if you're going to get one thing right, start at the foundation. As we grow, we will continue to deliver more lines of quality products that make you excited to wear Everyday. More everyday essentials are on the way.

"Start at the foundation. Get that right, and everything else follows."
